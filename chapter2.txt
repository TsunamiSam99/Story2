Walking relentlessly through a sunbathed desert. Yeah, it totally sucked.
Of course this didn't stop us from heading straight into the first saloon we could find to refresh ourselves with crudely-made rye whiskey, "No ice."
"Sorry we're all out!" joked the bartender. Obviously, it wasn't very funny to the tortiose who was still scratching at his shell rash.
The bartender wasn't familiar with shell rash, and thankfully didn't ask.
